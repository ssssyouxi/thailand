ABOUT LIMING

  • A grinding plant manufacturer

    Liming Heavy Industry is an outstanding grinding supplier integrating research&development, producing and sales together. Based on more than 30 years of experiences, the company has launched many grinding machines including coarse powder mill, micro powder mill, vertical mill and Raymond mill. From product customization to equipment supply, there is no complex transaction and middleman in this cooperation. And we can supply equipment by water carriage or land carriage.

  • A strategic partner of siemens

    Focusing on quality of products, Liming Heavy Industry chooses high quality raw materials and bearings from famous brand as soon as the production starts. Besides, as the strategic partner of siemens, we will select siemens motor for grinding machines, which can make this grinding production line stable and steady.

  • The common choice of 130 countries

    Since the products made in Liming Heavy Industry launched to market, these machines have won high praises from global customers because of advantages of stable running, high efficiency and simple maintenance method. At present, the products have sold to Thailand, Indonesia, Kenya, Chile, etc. 130 countries, which have been good choices for grinding production line.

ABOUT EQUIPMENT

  • TGM Trapezium Mill

    reliable quality, advanced design, D50

    TGM Trapezium Mill is an economical and cost-effective grinding plant. This machine adopts new design of grinding roller and grinding ring to improve capacity and size of product. It takes advantages of long service life and low vibration noise.

  • MTW European type Mill

    large capacity, maintenance friendly, green process

    MTW European type Mill is a patent and advanced product. It adopts many technologies consisting of bevel gear drive, internal dilute oil lubrication system and curve duct. And it is equipped with advanced dust wiper which can improve production efficiency and reduce energy consumption.
